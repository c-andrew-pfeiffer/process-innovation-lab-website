About

The Process Innovation Lab is where continuous-improvement craft meets the innovation and AI era.

Continuous improvement isn't one discipline. It spans design thinking, operational excellence, and intelligent automation. The Lab works across all three.

Most CI professionals are caught between two pressures: innovation is changing how the work gets done, and good process thinking matters more than ever. Senior practitioners are working out where the new tools fit. Early-career analysts are strong on tools but still building craft. This is the space in between.

Across 25 years in process improvement, I build products and coaching that help you orchestrate innovation without losing the craft underneath it.

No consulting engagements. No frameworks for their own sake. Just practical learning you can apply.

What innovation adds to the craft

Craft without innovation
  • Reach capped by your own hours
  • Synthesis and first-pass analysis by hand
  • Operator: every step is yours to run
  • Trusted work, slower than the pace demands
Craft with innovation
  • Reach extended past your own hours
  • Synthesis and first-pass analysis carried by the tools
  • Orchestrator: you direct, the judgment stays yours
  • Trusted work, at the pace the work demands
